The Beauty of Typography

Typography is the art of arranging text to make written language readable and appealing. It involves the careful selection of font families, sizes, and line spacing to ensure the content looks both attractive and legible.

Great typography creates a sense of harmony and rhythm on a page. The font weight adds contrast, while proper letter-spacing ensures smooth flow. Every designer knows how important these small details are.

The first letter and first line of this article have been styled using pseudo-elements for visual emphasis. Typography truly shapes how we experience information.
